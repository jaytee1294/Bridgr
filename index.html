FAQ
Common questions.
Does Bridgr work with my prop firm?+
Bridgr supports Tradeify, Apex Trader Funding, Topstep, Take Profit Trader, Bulenox, and FundedNext at launch. If your firm uses Tradovate or Rithmic as the executing broker, Bridgr will work with it.
Do I need to know how to code?+
Not at all. Bridgr's alert builder generates your complete JSON payload automatically. If your TradingView alert is set up, you can be live in under 10 minutes with zero code written.
How does the prop firm guard work?+
You enter your account size and select your prop firm. Bridgr loads the firm's exact rules — daily loss limit, consistency requirements, max contracts. As your P&L updates throughout the day, Bridgr monitors in real time and blocks any new orders that would breach your limits.
When does Bridgr launch?+
We're currently in private development. Founding members get access first — we'll reach out personally to every founding member before the public launch to get their setup running perfectly.
Is the founding member discount really forever?+
Yes. 30% off, locked in at signup, no expiry. The people who bet on us early deserve to be rewarded permanently — not just for an introductory period.
How is this different from PickMyTrade?+
PickMyTrade is a general-purpose webhook relay with zero prop firm awareness. Bridgr is built specifically for funded traders — with daily loss limit enforcement, consistency cap tracking, session filters, and duplicate alert protection. And it starts at $29/mo vs PMT's $50/mo.
