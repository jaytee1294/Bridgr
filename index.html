FAQ
Common questions.
Does Bridgr work with my prop firm?+
Bridgr supports Tradeify, Apex Trader Funding, Topstep, Take Profit Trader, Bulenox, and FundedNext at launch. If your firm uses Tradovate or Rithmic as the executing broker, Bridgr will work with it.
How is this different from TradeSyncer?+
TradeSyncer has zero prop firm awareness — no daily loss limit protection, no consistency tracking, no payout tracker, no test mode. Bridgr is built specifically for funded traders. And it starts at $39/mo vs TradeSyncer's $50/mo.
How does the prop firm guard work?+
You select your prop firm and account size. Bridgr loads the firm's exact rules — daily loss limit, consistency requirements, max contracts. As your P&L updates in real time, Bridgr monitors and blocks any new copy orders that would breach your limits.
What is the one-click test trade?+
Hit the test button in your dashboard and Bridgr fires a paper trade to verify your connection is working. You see the result in seconds — no more waiting days for a live signal to fire just to find out your setup was broken.
When does Bridgr launch?+
We're currently in private development. Founding members get access first — we'll reach out personally to every founding member before the public launch to get their setup running.
Is the founding member discount really forever?+
Yes. 30% off, locked in at signup, no expiry. The people who bet on us early deserve to be rewarded permanently.
